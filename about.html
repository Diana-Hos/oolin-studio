about oolin studio

concept

oolin studio creates tufted wool carpets inspired by both the venerated Iranian carpet and science-fiction. Lightly touching the history of the Iranian carpet, we borrow from the traditional regional motifs and color palettes, while integrating new elements and perspectives. We are also curious about science-fiction as a methodology of futurology and a mechanism that materializes the ‘other’ as alien. Integrating the figure of the alien, or goddess, we begin to explore and experiment with the concept of ornamental futurism in craft.

customization

oolin studio understands the individual home aesthetic and thus offers an element of customization. The carpet designs come in three iterations and we will allow small changes that can be requested in the order form. We will try our best to work with our clients to fit their aesthetic needs whle maintaining the integrity of the original carpet designs.

artist

oolin studio is based in toronto/tkaronto and led by canadian-iranian visual artist diana hos.
